Banana Bread

Description

Exactly what it sounds like: bread made out of bananas!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1&1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1&1/2 cups mashed bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil or melted butter

Steps

  1. Grease bottom and 1/2 inch up the sides of one 9x5x3-inch or two 7&1/2x3&1/2x2-inch loaf pans; set aside. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and 1/4 tsp salt. Make a well in center of flour mixture; set aside
  2. In a medium bowl combine eggs, bananas, sugar and olive oil/butter. Add egg mixture all at once to flour mixture. Stir just until moistened. Spoon batter into prepared pan(s)
  3. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 55 to 60 minutes for the one pan, or 40 to 45 minutes for two. Cool in pan on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Remove from pan. Cool completely on a wire rack. Wrap and store overnight before slicing
  4. Bon Appetit!
